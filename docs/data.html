Redd Counts and Environmental Covariates by Core Area and Population

Published

March 14, 2026

Each section below corresponds to one core area. Use the table of contents on the right to jump to a core area or local population of interest.

For each population, plots show:

  • Redd counts — bars = population total, points = individual survey sites
  • Summer streamflow — log(annual summer discharge); actual calendar year of measurement (effective lag = 6 yr in model)
  • August temperature — mean August water temperature (°C); actual calendar year of measurement (effective lag = 6 yr in model)
  • Non-native trout ratio — proportion of non-native trout catch (brown, brook, rainbow)

Each core area also shows the lake trout abundance index (core-level covariate).
